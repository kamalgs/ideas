The Shit Spiral — LLMs Training on LLM Output

research 1 March 2026
aillmtraining-datawebcontent-qualitymedia

As high-quality content gets walled off, the crawlable web skews toward AI-generated filler, SEO farms, marketing copy, and social media noise. Each training generation ingests a worse ratio. Models trained on this output produce more of it. That gets scraped. The next generation is worse.

The ouroboros of mediocrity — LLMs trained on LLM-generated LinkedIn slop, confidently producing more of it.

The bifurcation: high-value LLM capability moves to licensed enterprise pipelines (Bloomberg + LLM), proprietary corpus models, and user-provided context. The general-purpose consumer LLM becomes expensive autocomplete for mediocre content.

The framing that stuck: The open web as training substrate is probably a 2015–2024 phenomenon.
